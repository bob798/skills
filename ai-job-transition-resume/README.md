# AI Job Transition Resume Skill

> Optimize your resume for AI engineer / AI pre-sales / AI product roles in the Chinese job market.

Unlike general resume optimization, this skill focuses on the **unique hiring logic of AI roles**: interviewers don't care about your job title history — they want to see **real AI projects you built from scratch**.

---

## When to Use

Trigger this skill when you're:

- Transitioning from a traditional tech or non-tech role to AI
- A student applying for AI engineer / AI product positions
- Struggling to present AI experience when you "don't have enough"
- Applying to roles requiring RAG, Agent, MCP, LangChain experience

---

## What It Does

- Diagnoses your resume against AI job JD requirements
- Rewrites project descriptions with AI-specific tech stack framing
- Restructures skills section for AI roles (LLM / RAG / Agent / Tools)
- Provides recovery strategies when you lack AI projects
- Matches your resume keywords to specific JD requirements

---

## Core Insight

```
No AI projects = No competitive resume, regardless of years of experience.
1 working GitHub project > 5 years of "AI experience" without proof.
```

---

## Use Cases

- `ai engineer resume` — backend/fullstack developer transitioning to AI
- `ai pre-sales resume` — consultants/PMs moving to AI solution selling
- `ai resume no experience` — fresh grads or career changers with limited AI background
- `jd match analysis` — checking if resume keywords match a specific job posting
